Reverse Engineer of Electric Screwdriver

Introduction

The assignment was to create a device that would utilize NiChrome wire to cut through foam. This project was for ME360: Product Design. I worked in a group of 4 and my main contribution was with the design of the product and the soldering of the wires inside the device. The device is made of two 3D printed halves that are held together by four 5-40 screws and nuts.

Initial Sketch

CAD Drawings

Results

The device worked perfectly and could be completely dissasembled and reassembled without damaging the product. The NiChrome was placed inside a clay cast to isolate the heat from the plastic body of the device.

Areas of Improvement

An area of improvement for this product would be the surface finish of the product. This was 3D printed using ABS plastic, but if we were to machine this using traditional manufacturing using an NC mill, we could make this very comfortable in the hand.

Learning Moments

From this project, I learned how great group projects can be if you have the right team. I was in a team of four mechanical engineers. Everyone arrived to meetings five minutes early and prepared to get the work done. Because of this, we made a very clean and functional product and were able to make a few prototypes to learn from.
